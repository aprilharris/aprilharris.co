Bio

April Harris is freelance web developer and CSS enthusiast based in NYC. She holds a bachelor of arts in history from CUNY Hunter College.

Originally self taught, April began tinkering around with HTML and CSS in 2016. She decided to formalize her training and enrolled in a coding bootcamp in 2018. She joined America On Tech in 2019.

April enjoys web development, and CSS in particular, because it allows her to merge her interests in design and technology.

When she's not coding, you can find April spending time with her family, doing some DIY home improvement, or using her sewing machine.
